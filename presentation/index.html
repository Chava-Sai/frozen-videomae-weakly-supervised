Training Recipe

3 tricks that made results reproducible across all 3 random seeds

01
Inverse-Frequency Weights
wc = N / (C × nc)
  • 🔴 Shooting: 1.83×
  • 🔴 Explosion: 2.41×
  • ⚪ Normal: 0.06×
  • Prevents "all Normal" collapse
  • Critical for minority classes
02
Confidence Threshold τ = 0.30
Filter noisy pseudo-labels
  • Only high-confidence predictions
  • Applied during MIL bag sampling
  • Reduces false-positive signal
  • Tuned on validation split
  • Early epochs benefit most
03
Cosine Annealing LR
η : 1×10⁻⁴ → 1×10⁻⁶ (40 epochs)
  • No oscillations or divergence
  • 3-epoch warm-up
  • Adam optimizer
  • Weight decay = 1×10⁻⁴
  • Stable across all 3 seeds
Result: 3-seed std AUC = ±0.013 — Results are robust and reproducible

Temporal Localization Breakthrough

Why such a large jump? VideoMAE learns precise spatiotemporal boundaries via masked patch reconstruction — tokens are inherently sensitive to motion onset & cessation. I3D optical-flow features blur across temporal boundaries, explaining poor localization at strict IoU thresholds.
